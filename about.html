What I Love Doing

Hi, I’m Nida, a UI/UX and web designer based in Santa Clara, CA. I focus on creating clean, user-friendly digital experiences that are both functional and visually thoughtful. I love turning complex ideas into intuitive interfaces through wireframing, prototyping, and visual design.

I’ve worked on websites, digital content, and user-centered design projects for colleges, studios, and nonprofits. Tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and HTML/CSS are part of my everyday process, helping me build interactive designs that feel seamless across devices.

I’m currently finishing my degree in Film and Digital Media at College of San Mateo. Studying storytelling and visual communication has helped me approach design from both a creative and strategic point of view. I'm passionate about making digital spaces more usable, inclusive, and engaging.
